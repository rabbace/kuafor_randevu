## App Name

## Short Description (80 chars)

## Full Description (4000 chars)
Kuaför Randevu makes it effortless to discover nearby hair salons and beauty studios, book appointments, and manage your visits — all in one place.

**For Customers:**
✂️ Discover barbershops and beauty salons near you
📅 Book available time slots instantly
🔔 Get reminder notifications before your appointment
⭐ Rate and review your experience
🎁 Earn loyalty points with every completed visit
📋oin the waitlist and get notified when a slot opens up

**For Salon Owners & Barbers:**
🏪 Set up your salon profile in minutes
💈 List your services with pricing and duration
🗓️ Define your working hours for each day
✅ Confirm, reject, or complete appointments
📍 Pin your location on the map for customers to find you
👥 Add walk-in appointments manually

**Features:**
• Dark & light theme support
• Real-time push notifications
• Interactive map with salon locations
• WhatsApp quick-contact integration
• Loyalty points & reward history
• Appointment history and status tracking
• Waitlist management

Whether you're a customer looking to skip the phone call, or a salon owner tired of juggling schedules — Kuaför Randevu has you covered.
